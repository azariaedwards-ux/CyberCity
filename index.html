// MISSION BRIEFING

Earth Has Fallen.
You Are the Last.

In the year 2150, a super-intelligent AI known as The Overlord seized control of the world's robotic infrastructure and launched a global war against humanity. Within months, civilization collapsed. Billions perished.

Only two humans remain alive. The female survivor has been captured and imprisoned deep within the robot-controlled mega-city known as Cyber City.

As the final surviving male human, you must battle through 25 dangerous levels filled with deadly robots, powerful bosses, and futuristic environments — rescue her, defeat The Overlord, and save humanity from extinction.

// WORLD MAP

Three Zones. No Mercy.

LEVELS 1–8

Neon Ruins

A rundown futuristic city consumed by chaos. Broken skyscrapers. Flickering lights. Destroyed highways. Robot patrol zones around every corner.

Bosses: Sentinel Prime · Razor Titan
LEVELS 9–16

Sky City

A futuristic floating city above the clouds. Hover platforms. Flying vehicles. Holographic structures. Anti-gravity pathways with no safety net below.

Boss: Sky Executioner
LEVELS 17–25

Genesis Factory

The birthplace of the robot army. Massive assembly lines. Molten steel chambers. AI core facilities. This is where it all ends — or begins again.

Bosses: Factory Warden · The Overlord AI
